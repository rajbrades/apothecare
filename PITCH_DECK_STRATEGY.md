## 2. Team Audit: The Current State

### 🗣️ Communications Audit
*   **Strength:** The "Deep Consult" and "Agentic AI" terminology is strong. It differentiates us from "just another chatbot."
*   **Opportunity:** The *Supplement Brand Preference* feature is a massive business lever (marketplace revenue), but it's currently buried in features. For investors, this needs to be a headline revenue strategy.
*   **Gap:** We need to clearly separate "Scribe" (commodity) from "CDSS" (high value). The pitch must position Scribe as the *hook*, and CDSS as the *retention*.

### 🎨 Graphic Design Audit
*   **Identity:** The existing app design (Newsreader Serif + DM Sans + Teal/Gold) is excellent. It signals "Academic Journal meets Silicon Valley."
*   **Deck Vibe:** The pitch deck should mirror the app’s "Dark Mode" elegance. Use the specific hex codes from `globals.css` (`--color-brand-950` for backgrounds, `--color-gold-500` for key stats).
*   **Visual Hook:** We need to visualize the **IFM Matrix**. <br> **The "Living Matrix":** A slide showing the 7-node matrix "lighting up" as data flows in is our "money shot."
*   **"Time Travel" Timeline:** Visualizing the patient's entire life history in one click.

### 🚀 Social/Growth Audit
*   **The Viral Loop:** The *Patient Education Studio* (NotebookLM style) is our secret weapon. Every PDF or Video generated is a marketing asset.
*   **Strategy:** For the pitch, do not just list "Marketing." Show "Network Effects." When a practitioner sends a protocol to a patient, that patient sees Apothecare branding.
