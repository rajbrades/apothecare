# Using NotebookLM to Accelerate Your Pitch Deck

Since you want to use **NotebookLM** (Google), here is the specific workflow to turn your markdown files into a pitch.

## 1. What NotebookLM Will Do For You

*   **Deepen the Content:** It will "read" your Business Plan and Pitch Strategies and allow you to ask questions like "What are the top 3 objections an investor might have?"
*   **Generate Speaker Scripts:** Turn the bullet points into a natural, conversational script.
*   **Create Audio Overviews:** Generate a "Podcast" version of your pitch to send to investors beforehand (a unique touch!).

## 2. Step-by-Step Workflow

### Step A: Load Your "Brain"
1.  Go to [notebooklm.google.com](https://notebooklm.google.com).
2.  Create a New Notebook called **"Apotheca Pitch"**.
3.  **Upload Sources:** Upload the following files we created (you may need to copy-paste the text if it doesn't accept .md directly, or convert to PDF first):
    *   `BUSINESS_PLAN.md`
    *   `PITCH_DECK_INVESTORS.md`
    *   `PITCH_DECK_ADVISORS.md`
    *   `docs/PRD-visits.md`

### Step B: Refine the Pitch (The "Chat" Phase)
Ask NotebookLM these prompts to sharpen your deck:

*   **"Critique my Investor Deck. What is the weakest slide?"**
    *   *Why:* NotebookLM will analyze the logic gaps.
*   **"Generate a 30-second elevator pitch based on the Executive Summary."**
    *   *Why:* Perfect for the "Title Slide" talk track.
*   **"Create a list of FAQ answers for the 'Business Model' slide."**
    *   *Why:* Be ready for Q&A about the Marketplace revenue.

### Step C: The "Audio Overview" (The Viral Asset)
1.  Click **"Generate Audio Overview"** (the Deep Dive podcast feature).
2.  **Listen:** It will create a 5-10 minute conversation between two AI hosts discussing Apotheca.
3.  **Use It:**
    *   **Internal Review:** Listen to it yourself to hear how your business sounds "from the outside."
    *   **Investor Teaser:** Send the audio link *before* the meeting. "Here’s a 5-min AI-generated podcast about what we’re building." (Meta, since your product is AI).

### Step D: Build the Visuals (NotebookLM doesn't do visuals yet)
NotebookLM handles the **logic and audio**. You still need a visual tool for the slides.
*   Use the **Outputs** from NotebookLM (refined scripts, FAQs) as the text for your slides in **Slides/Keynote/Figma**.

## Summary
Use NotebookLM as your **Co-Founder** to stress-test the logic and create the audio teaser. Use **Figma/Slides** to build the visual deck.
