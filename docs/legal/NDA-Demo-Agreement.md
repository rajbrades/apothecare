## 1. PURPOSE

The Company is providing the Recipient with a confidential demonstration of the Apothecare platform — an AI-powered clinical decision support system for functional and integrative medicine practitioners ("Platform"). This Agreement governs the Recipient's obligations regarding all Confidential Information disclosed during or in connection with this demonstration.

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means all non-public information disclosed by the Company to the Recipient, whether orally, visually, in writing, or in electronic form, including but not limited to:

- (a) The Platform's features, functionality, user interface, user experience, workflows, and design;
- (b) Software architecture, algorithms, AI models, prompts, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines, data schemas, and source code;
- (c) Business plans, strategies, pricing, partnerships, customer lists, and go-to-market plans;
- (d) Clinical content, evidence databases, partnership integrations, and proprietary knowledge bases;
- (e) Any screenshots, recordings, notes, or materials created by the Recipient based on or derived from the demonstration;
- (f) The existence and terms of this Agreement.

Confidential Information does not include information that: (i) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Recipient; (ii) was known to the Recipient prior to disclosure, as documented in writing; (iii) is independently developed by the Recipient without use of or reference to the Confidential Information; or (iv) is disclosed to the Recipient by a third party without restriction.

## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECIPIENT

The Recipient agrees to:

- (a) **Not disclose** any Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Company;
- (b) **Not reproduce, copy, or duplicate** the Platform or any of its features, workflows, designs, or functionality, in whole or in part;
- (c) **Not develop, build, or assist in building** any product, service, or feature that is substantially similar to or derived from the Confidential Information;
- (d) **Not record, screenshot, photograph, or capture** any portion of the demonstration unless expressly authorized in writing by the Company;
- (e) **Limit access** to Confidential Information to those individuals within the Recipient's organization who have a need to know and who are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as those in this Agreement;
- (f) **Protect** the Confidential Information using at least the same degree of care used to protect the Recipient's own confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care;
- (g) **Promptly notify** the Company of any unauthorized disclosure or use of Confidential Information.

## 4. NO LICENSE OR RIGHTS GRANTED

Nothing in this Agreement grants the Recipient any license, right, or interest in the Platform, its intellectual property, trade secrets, or any related technology. All rights, title, and interest in and to the Confidential Information remain exclusively with the Company.

## 5. NO OBLIGATION

This Agreement does not obligate either party to proceed with any business relationship, transaction, investment, or partnership. The Company reserves the right to terminate access to any demonstration or materials at any time, for any reason.

## 6. NON-SOLICITATION

For a period of twelve (12) months following the date of this Agreement, the Recipient shall not, directly or indirectly, solicit, recruit, or hire any employee, contractor, or consultant of the Company who was involved in the demonstration or development of the Platform.

## 7. TERM AND SURVIVAL

This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of **three (3) years** from the Effective Date. The Recipient's obligations with respect to trade secrets shall survive indefinitely, to the extent permitted by applicable law.

## 8. RETURN OR DESTRUCTION

Upon written request by the Company, the Recipient shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information in its possession, including any copies, notes, summaries, or derivative materials, and shall certify such return or destruction in writing.

## 9. REMEDIES

The Recipient acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm to the Company for which monetary damages would be an inadequate remedy. The Company shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief, specific performance, or other equitable remedies in addition to any other remedies available at law, without the requirement of posting a bond.

## 10. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Florida, without regard to its conflict of laws principles. Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved exclusively in the state or federal courts located in the State of Florida.

## 11. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous oral or written agreements. This Agreement may not be amended except in writing signed by both parties.
